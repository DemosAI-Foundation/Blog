DemosAI is a foundation in Finland, that furthers education and awareness of all things AI, AGI etc. to empower the people!

It is founded by Benjamin Bonn, who has been interested in artificial intelligence since he first started playing video games.
He always wondered what the characters would do, once you as the player left the game. He proceeded to design a multiple choice excel document for what characters would do in each situation at 14 years old in Warcraft setting, spanning their daily routine to life goals, some of them were based on probabilities or even ancestry!

Benjamin has contributed to games such as Battlefield with key design, written a Sci-fi novel, now working as a consultant in the software industry. As a consultant he has created many LLM or machine learning powered systems for clients.
